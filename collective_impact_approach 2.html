The vision that we have developed with our stakeholders maintains the heart of our history of building thriving communities, while recognizing that the complexity of the issues currently facing these communities requires a new approach.

Our mission statement describes this approach as Collective Impact, “a disciplined approach for building cross-sector partnerships that transform systems.”

The strategic plan we follow builds on our recent successes partnering with organizations from different sectors to innovate around the most pressing issues facing Philadelphia’s communities.

The Collective Impact Model offers a clear structure for formalizing this new approach, allowing FNC and its partners to work together more effectively for larger impact.

Neighborhood Centers, the centerpiece of the historic approach of FNC, will continue to be a core set of partners as FNC strives to bring a wider, more diverse group of stakeholders to our team.
